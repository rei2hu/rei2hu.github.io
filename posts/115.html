on dreaming

Dreaming. I don't do it much, or at least I don't remember it when I wake up, but last night I had a very calming experience.

I won't bother with the details but it ended/I woke up at a peaceful moment and felt extremely relaxed.

Considering what I've been going through over the past few weeks, it was a much needed reprieve; I literally thanked my mind for the dream when I awoke. It was so impactful that I decided to write it down.

I considered extending this post by talking about things like lucid dreaming or other sleep practices (none of which I do), but in the end I decided to keep this short and sweet.
